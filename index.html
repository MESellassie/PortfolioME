Shift2Success: Pathway to College & Career Fast Tracking

A skills training initiative targeting low-income high schoolers and young adults in the state of Alabama, focusing on manufacturing certification and financial literacy.

PROCESS
  • Planning and design of website theme, UI and UX.
  • Coordination with the National Association of Manufacturers, the Alabama Community College System, and various Alabama based community colleges to formalize content language, imagery, and promotions.
  • Direct push to local Alabama media outlets specializing in education, skills training, or manufacturing within the state.
  • Development of social media campaign and press kit for sharing with organizational partners to ensure coordinated social media launch and promotion.

Website theme, UI and UX design

The organization requested a splash page look that combined themes of diversity student training with a historical context. I brought in stock imagery of students of color in manufacturing and created a background image using W.E.B. DuBois and Booker T. Washington to represent the historical context.

Local media coordination

Direct outreach to several media outlets in Alabama, including local college papers at the partner sites for the project, led to the publication of local articles about the program's launch. Click here for an example of one such article.
